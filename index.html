About BeirutPoly

BeirutPoly is a satirical board game based on the beloved Monopoly, featuring real-life Beirut neighborhoods, cultural references, and hilarious Lebanese twists. Navigate blackouts, Wasta, inflation, and more as you compete to be the kingpin of Beirut real estate.
